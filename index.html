How did you start making music?

Timeline: Late 2023 - Early 2024.

I was playing a rhythm game called "Funky Friday" on Roblox and discovered an artist named Camellia. His chaotic, complex style blew my mind and inspired me to open up a DAW and try making my own music.

What are your hobbies?

When I'm not producing in FL Studio, I love:

  • Fishing
  • Riding Dirt Bikes and ATVs
  • Astrophotography (taking pictures of space)
  • Video Editing & Short Films

"I'm open to trying almost anything!"

Tell me about the Album

Multiversal Transcend is my first major album. It serves as a bridge between genres.

Expect a fusion of raw speed (Speedcore) and cinematic atmosphere. The goal is not just music, but a narrative experience.

What is "Full Flavor"?

My style is "Full Flavor" because I refuse to be locked into a single genre. If it sounds good, I make it.
